Single Player Game

Roll the dice to get a score. You win if you score

Double Player game

Player1 goes first and can roll the dice to get a
score of over 20, once this has been done then
the player has won.
If the player rolls a 1 before they reach 20
the player has lost. However before reaching 20
the player can save their score by clicking 'hold'
Once this is done control of the game has gone to
player2 and will stay with this player until they
have clicked 'hold' or has won the game
